The most powerful tool in the lab — shown as an owned, sanitized demo on **synthetic data**.
It educates on what the product is and how it flexes; takes in many planning factors; maps a
full revenue accounting to all parties; carries the process and operational flow; positions the
"what's in it for me" Benefit-Advantage-Feature story; bundles reference documents; generates
sales ideas and presentations; and monitors compliance throughout.

Carrier math and fee logic are **generalized** in the public demo — the method and the build,
never proprietary carrier IP or real records.
